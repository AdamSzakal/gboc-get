‹ Balaenoptera

Beskrivning

Balaenoptera is the sloper traverse above the "I väntan" problem, which is opposite to "Cuerpo de hombre". It starts on the very left on the last crimp before the sloperrail begins, and then continues to the right on the rail until you reach the obvious end jug. The jug is followed by an easy topout.

Grad: 8B

Betyg: ★★★
